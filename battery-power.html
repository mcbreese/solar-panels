Battery Efficiency (%):Enter the efficiency of the battery for the potential system

Battery Capacity (A Hours):Enter the battery capacity that will be used for the potential system
Battery Voltage (V):Enter the voltage of the battery for the potential system

Breakdown:

1. To calculate the total power output by the battery charge module (W):

efficiency x sun power (1000) x roof area x battery efficiency x sin(roof angle + sun angle)

2. To calculate the constant base load of power on the house (W):

power consumption + ((1- power distribution module efficiency) x power consumption)

3. To calculate the power balance of the house (W):

power output of charge module - power consumption of house

4. To calculate the instantaneous charge or discharge current (A):

power balance / battery voltage

5. To calculate the battery charge at each hour

battery charge at the hour + charge or discharge current

6. To calculate the % discharge of the battery:

(1 - (battery charge / battery capacity) x 100
